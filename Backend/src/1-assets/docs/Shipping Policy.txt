SHIPPING & DELIVERY POLICY
Last Updated: 2025

This Shipping & Delivery Policy (“Policy”) describes how Northwind Traders (“Company”, “we”, “us”, “our”) handles the shipping, delivery, and logistics of products purchased through the Pet Univerzum platform (“Services”).

By placing an order, you agree to the terms outlined in this Policy.

1. DELIVERY AREAS
1.1 Deliveries are currently available within the territory of the Republic of Serbia.  
1.2 Express in-house delivery may be available in selected cities.  
1.3 International shipping is not supported at this time unless explicitly stated.

2. SHIPPING PARTNERS
2.1 We cooperate with licensed courier services operating in Serbia.  
2.2 Courier assignments may vary depending on location, product type, and availability.

3. DELIVERY TIMES
3.1 Estimated delivery times are displayed during checkout.  
3.2 Delivery time may vary due to:  
– courier workload,  
– weather conditions,  
– holidays,  
– product availability,  
– operational or technical delays.  

3.3 These estimates are not guaranteed.

4. SHIPPING FEES
4.1 Shipping fees are calculated based on:  
– delivery address,  
– courier pricing,  
– product weight and size.  
4.2 Shipping fees are shown before finalizing the order.

5. ORDER PROCESSING
5.1 Orders placed before the daily cutoff time are processed the same day.  
5.2 Orders placed after cutoff time or on holidays may be processed the next business day.

6. FAILED DELIVERY ATTEMPTS
6.1 If the courier is unable to deliver due to incorrect address, absence of the recipient, or other user-caused issues, additional delivery attempts may incur extra fees.  
6.2 If the package is returned to us, the user may be responsible for reshipping costs.

7. RISK OF LOSS
7.1 Risk passes to the user once the package is handed to the courier.  
7.2 We are not responsible for delays, damage, or loss caused by courier services.

8. DAMAGED OR INCORRECT DELIVERIES
8.1 Users must report damaged, missing, or incorrect items within:  
– 48 hours for damaged items,  
– 72 hours for incorrect items,  
– 5 business days for missing packages.  

8.2 Claims must include photos or video evidence when applicable.

9. SPECIAL CONDITIONS FOR PET FOOD AND PERISHABLE ITEMS
9.1 Certain food products may require temperature-safe handling.  
9.2 The user is responsible for promptly receiving such packages.  
9.3 The Company is not responsible for spoilage caused by delayed pickup.
